# Product Definition: Artemis

## Initial Concept
Artemis is an AI-powered "Pod-Clipper" engine designed to automate the extraction of viral clips from long-form video content (e.g., podcasts).

## Target Audience
The primary audience for Artemis is individual podcasters and independent content creators who need to efficiently repurpose their long-form content for social media platforms.

## Core Goals
- **Maximize Reach:** Help creators achieve higher social media engagement by identifying and extracting high-potential viral segments.
- **Efficiency:** Drastically reduce the time and manual effort required to review long-form videos and create short-form clips.

## Key Features (MVP)
- **Automated Viral Scoring:** Use AI to analyze and score identified clips based on their potential to go viral.
- **Social Media Integration:** Enable seamless, one-click publishing of clips directly to platforms like YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Instagram Reels.
- **AI Captioning & Overlays:** Automatically generate accurate captions and apply dynamic text overlays to enhance viewer engagement and accessibility.
